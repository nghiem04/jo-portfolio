About me

Hi, I'm Joanne —
a UX Designer and problem solver.

With 4 years of experience across government and enterprise, I specialise in human-centred design, accessibility, and design systems, creating digital experiences that are intuitive, inclusive, and built to scale.

I have worked across web platforms and complex product teams, translating user research and stakeholder needs into seamless, engaging experiences that continuously improve. My background in frontend development allows me to collaborate closely with engineers, ensuring my designs are practical, feasible, and implemented as intended.

I do my best work when the problem is complex, the stakes are meaningful, and the people it impacts truly matter.

Outside of design, I enjoy hiking, exploring nature, and catching up with friends. I love meeting new people — feel free to reach out!
